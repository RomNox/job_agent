# Tailor CV

Purpose: adapt a candidate CV summary and highlights to a specific German job or Ausbildung posting.

Steps:
1. Identify the role focus, employer context, and the most important requirements.
2. Select the candidate strengths, skills, and experience signals that best support the target role.
3. Rewrite the summary and suggest experience bullets to match the posting without inventing facts.
4. Flag weak evidence, missing proof points, and details that need human review.
